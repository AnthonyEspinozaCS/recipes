Tinga

A classic mexican dish, featuring chicken, chipotle peppers, and onion!

A staple in my mother's repotoire. You can use this for sandwiches, tostadas, or just by itself.

Ingredients:

  • onion
  • chicken
  • chipotle peppers

Steps:

  1. boil chicken
  2. shred chicken
  3. toss chicken in chipotle peppers
